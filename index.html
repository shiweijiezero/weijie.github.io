Hi! I am a second year PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), advised by Prof. Xiaofang Zhou. Previously, I obtained my Master's degree from Soochow University (Suzhou, China), where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Jiajie Xu.

Research Interests

My research focuses on Large Language Models (LLMs), Data Management, Reinforcement Learning, and Spatial-Temporal Data.

I am particularly interested in efficient data management strategies for LLM training and applications, enhancing the reasoning capabilities and reliability of large language models through reinforcement learning and reflection mechanisms. Additionally, I continue to work on spatial-temporal data analytics, particularly in trajectory map matching and related applications in traffic systems.
